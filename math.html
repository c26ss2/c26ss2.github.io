Euchler problem 1:

If we list all the natural numbers below 10 that are multiples of 3 or 5, we get 3, 5, 6 and 9. The sum of these multiples is 23. Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000. the answer is... 233168!! to do it yourself, right click, click inspect on the menu, go to the console, and scroll down until you see zero. take the number right above that and add it with the number right above the next zero as well as the last negative number at the bottom of the console, and you will get the same answer!!
